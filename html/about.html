GetASlot is an online solution that allows individuals and companies to schedule appointments online. Clients can book appointments online by using any social platform like Facebook or Twitter and get reminders before an appointment begins. Users can implement appointment solution into their own website or use GetASlot website to promote their company with Google map, description and information about employees.
